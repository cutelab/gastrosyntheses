# tsatziki ish sauce

## ingredients
- greek yogurt (e.g. thick/strained yogurt)
- lemon juice
- olive oil
- salt
- herbs: e.g. parsley, dill
- 1/2 garlic clove, minced (less than you think)
- optional: cucumber

## directions
1. if using cucumber, peel, seed, and slice into thin matchsticks
1. chop herbs
1. mix everything together
