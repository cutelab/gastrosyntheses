# japanese salad dressing
from https://www.thespruceeats.com/sesame-salad-dressing-2031183

## Ingredients
- 3 tbsp white sesame seeds
- 2 tbsp rice vinegar
- 2 tbsp mayo
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp  sesame oil

## Instructions
Toast sesame seeds and grind them. Mix with the other ingredients
